Premier Catering

At Sanchez Tacos Catering, we’ll work closely with you to design and manage your event, from private business meetings and luncheons to large-scale conferences, employee appreciation events, grand openings and holiday parties. We offer a wide-range of flexible menus and custom options for different audiences. This way, you get what you want, when you need it, without all the added stress.

From our Inland Empire-based kitchen, we provide catering experiences across the area including San Diego County, Orange County, Los Angeles County, San Bernardino County, Riverside County and beyond.
